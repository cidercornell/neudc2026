Getting there

Getting to Ithaca

Ithaca Tompkins (ITH)

Closest airport. Direct regional flights from major hubs.

Syracuse (SYR)

~1 hr drive. Broader options, including low-cost carriers.

Rochester (ROC)

~1.5 hr drive. Additional flight options.

Newark (EWR)

~4 hr drive. Widest international connections.

FlixBus and OurBus

Bus from New York City to Ithaca (~5 hrs). Good option from EWR or JFK. FlixBus and OurBus.

# Register

**July 1 to October 17, 2026** · Payment through CVENT when registration opens.

The page opens with **Register** as the main heading: when registration is open and when fees will be posted. Below that: rate categories, reduced-rate eligibility, included meals, and a **Register now** control (inactive until registration opens).

## Registration

Registration will be open from July 1 through October 17, 2026.

The registration fees will be posted in dollars when registration opens on July 1, 2026.

### General rate

Faculty and professionals at universities and research institutions.

### Reduced rate

Eligible if you meet one of these criteria:

- PhD students
- Post-docs
- Researchers based in low- and middle-income countries
- Faculty at community colleges
- Faculty at historically Black colleges and universities (HBCUs)

### Meals

Welcome reception; breakfast and lunch on both full program days; dinner the first evening; coffee breaks.

---

## Travel grants

All researchers based in low- and middle-income countries who are accepted to present a political economy and governance-focused paper will be automatically considered for a travel grant through J-PAL's Governance Initiative. No separate application is required.

For questions about travel support, please [contact us](mailto:tmt1@cornell.edu).

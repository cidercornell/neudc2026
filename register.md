## Registration

Registration opens approximately July 1, 2026 and closes around October 17, 2026. Payment is handled through CVENT.

## Registration Fees

*Based on prior NEUDC conferences. Final fees confirmed when registration opens.*

### Faculty & Professionals (General)
**$300** per person

For faculty, researchers, and professionals at universities and research institutions.

*Registration opening July 2026*

### Students, Post-docs & LMIC Researchers (Reduced Rate)
**$250** per person

For PhD students, post-docs, LMIC-based researchers, and faculty at community colleges and HBCUs.

*Registration opening July 2026*

Fees cover breakfast both days, lunch on Day 1, coffee breaks, and the Saturday evening reception.

## Travel Grants

All LMIC-based researchers accepted to present a political economy and governance-focused paper will be automatically considered for a travel grant through J-PAL's Governance Initiative. No separate application is required.

For questions about travel support, please [contact us](mailto:tmt1@cornell.edu).

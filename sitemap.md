# Site Map

## `index.html` (Home)

- **Header/Nav:** Home, Register, Travel & Venue.
- **Hero:** NEUDC 2026 branding, dates/location, Call for Papers CTA (jumps to `#cfp`), Register CTA.
- **What is NEUDC?:** Conference overview text.
- **Key dates:** Dedicated section with CFP open, registration open/close, submission deadline, decisions, conference dates.
- **Program & keynotes:** Schedule placeholder section.
- **Keynotes & speakers:** Placeholder speaker cards (`Name TBA`) in a horizontal loop.
- **Call for papers (`#cfp`):** CFP timing plus Editorial Express submit CTA.
- **Footer:** Conference metadata and contact email (`tmt1@cornell.edu`).

## `register.html` (Register)

- **Header/Nav:** Home, Register, Travel & Venue.
- **Top status banner:** “Registration is not open yet” notice with July 1, 2026 opening date.
- **Intro:** Registration window and fee timing.
- **Fee table:** General vs Reduced rate categories.
- **Reduced eligibility list:** PhD students, post-docs, LMIC researchers, community college faculty, HBCU faculty.
- **Included with fee:** Reception, meals, coffee breaks.
- **Checkout status:** Disabled “Register now” button with July 1, 2026 opening note and CVENT note.
- **Footer:** Conference metadata and contact email.

## `travel.html` (Travel & Venue)

- **Header/Nav:** Home, Register, Travel & Venue.
- **Page intro + quick links:** Getting to Ithaca, Venue, Getting around, Parking, Lodging.
- **Getting to Ithaca:** Airport comparison table (ITH, SYR, JFK, EWR) with travel time and one-line notes.
- **Venue:** Physical Sciences Building details, directions/accessibility links, venue photo.
- **Getting around:**
  - **TCAT:** One-line definition, closest stop for NEUDC, payment options, transit resource links.
  - **Campus-to-Campus bus:** Ithaca–NYC coach info, booking/schedule links, contact details, photo.
- **Parking:** Short tips list plus visitor/short-term/permit links.
- **Lodging:** Placeholder note (hotel block info by summer 2026).
- **Footer:** Conference metadata and contact email.

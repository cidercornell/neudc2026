What is NEUDC?

NEUDC has organized annual conferences on development economics since 1967, making it the longest-running gathering in the field. Each year the conference rotates among member universities across the northeastern United States.

The conference brings together researchers, faculty, policymakers, and graduate students from across the globe to present and discuss cutting-edge research addressing the most pressing challenges facing developing economies. It is known for its rigorous academic standards, constructive feedback, and collaborative spirit.

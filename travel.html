Local transportation

TCAT Route 10 runs frequently between Cornell and downtown Ithaca, with a stop at Goldwin Smith Hall near the Physical Sciences Building. Pay with the Tfare app, a Tcard, or exact cash.

Parking at Cornell

Parking options vary by day and lot.

  • On weekdays, plan for a permit or paid visitor parking through ParkMobile or nearby lot kiosks.
  • On weekends, complimentary visitor parking is available at Toboggan Lodge, Forest Home Drive Garage, and Hoy Road Garage. Follow posted signage and restricted-space rules.
